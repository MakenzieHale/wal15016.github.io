A perfect day at the Geyser!

The Geyser in Soda Springs is the only captive geyser in history. Jenna Collins from our Perf Pic Weather team had the opportunity to go and explore the amazing town of Soda Springs, and here is what she had to report back: "Here in Soda Springs, the soda really is on them! This town is so full of rich history! I even learned that the town got its name thanks to past volcanic activity and bubbling gasses far beneath the earth- they really do have natural carbonated water springs! I enjoyed being able to learn about this cities history while here on my trip, the people here make you feel right at home and the hotel had impecable service. If you're looking to plan a unique trip, mark down Soda Springs on your list!" Thanks to the town of Soda Springs for continuing to use Perf Pic Weather as your continued weather provider!

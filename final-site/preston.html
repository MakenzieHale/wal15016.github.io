A Day At The Preston Orchard

U-Pick apple day at this local Preston Orchard was a hit this year considering the health crises among us. People came from all over to take a load off and enjoy in the fall festivities that this Orchard had to offer. The weather was said to be prestine, considering the time of year. The apples were primed for picking and now families in the local Preston area get to enjoy some homemade, warm apple pie-YUMMY! The owner of this orchard sent in this picture of their beautiful orchard along with a message of how grateful they were for all who attended, they felt so much joy and love.

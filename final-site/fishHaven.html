Exploring Fish Haven

Though the winter months have settled in, you can still explore the natural beauty of Fish Haven, Idaho! Fish Haven is a small town on the border of Bear Lake and is known for its' beauty and tranquility. Enjoy the quaint town of Fish Haven and the feeling of Zen while enjoying its' open lake.

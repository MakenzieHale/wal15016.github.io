Site Purpose:

The purpose of this site is to check the weather to see when the best times to take your pictures are. Too often when taking family pictures, we either dress too heavy or too lightly, making it a miserable and uncomfotrable time for all parties involved. This site will also help you decide on what time to schedule your pictures for in order to get the best lighting possible, as the site will tell you the current direction that the sun is pointing. Gone are the days of miserable photography sessions, it's finally time to get that PERF PIC!

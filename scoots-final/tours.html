Places to go, things to see!

Cozumel Sunsets

Cozumel is known for its beautiful and breath taking sunsets. Be sure to take a moment out of your adventurous day to stop and enjoy it.

Punta Sur Ecological Park

Punta Sur Eco Beach is our number one recommended spot to see! It is said to be the best beach on the island, located on the South shore-it covers 2,500 acres. It is home to exotic birds, crocodiles, sea turtles, flowers and beautiful beaches. This is an absolute must see! It is a perfect spot for couples renting our scooters, or groups renting our jeeps!

Celarain Lighthouse

The Celarain Lighthouse is another must see within the Punt Sur Eco Park. Constructed to warn sailors at night, it is now part of a maritime museum. It is 127 steps to the top to get 360Â° views of the Island, on a clear day you can even see the coast of Cuba! Make sure to check the weather on our home page!

Snorkeling

Take your rental to one of the various locations on the island that offers snorkeling tours! Be sure to look out for sea tutles, as most of our tourists get lucky enough to swim with some!
